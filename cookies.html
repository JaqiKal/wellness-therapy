About cookies

On our website, we apply cookies. Which are small text files from our web server stored on the visitor's computer. We use cookies to save your shopping cart and information. This helps provide you with the best website experience. Cookies are common and can be found on many different websites.

If you do not wish to store cookies, there are several ways for you to prevent it:

You can prevent your browser from storing cookies in its settings. Some websites may not work as well if you don't have cookies. You might lose some features. You cannot shop on our website. If there is a network disruption, any entered information will disappear. You will need to start again.

To get rid of cookies, you have to delete your personalized settings on certain websites.

Many newer browsers have a feature called private browsing or incognito browsing. This means that every time you close your browser, you delete all cookies.
If our website isn't working right, try clearing cookies to see if it improves.
