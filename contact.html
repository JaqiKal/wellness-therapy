Welcome!

At Manus Herba, we conduct a thorough assessment during the consultation. This includes reviewing your medical history, discussing your existing dietary and lifestyle practices, and checking if you are on any medications or supplements.

Gaining a comprehensive understanding of the potential causes of your illness is crucial for us. This knowledge allows us to formulate the most suitable therapy tailored to your specific health requirements and objectives.

A consultation begins with you receiving a questionnaire to fill out and send to me about a week before the visit. In order to find the cause of your problems and recommend therapy that can alleviate your specific issues, I need answers to some questions about you. When we meet, the consultation takes about an hour, and you will take home my written advice for you.
